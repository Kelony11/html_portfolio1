MY TOP 3 MARVEL MOVIES IN 2022/2023

Written by Kelvin Ihezue

Spiderman:Across the spiderverse(2023)

It had cool visuals and a very interesting storyline. I really cant wait for the final installment. Highly recommended!!

Black Panther(2022)

It was very emotional, 80 percent of the movie was dedicated to Chadwick boseman, the previous black pathner actor who died in 2019 as a result of colon cancer. The movie overall wasn't bad, it's just i dont really like the transition of a main character in a movie. It changes the whole experience for me.

Guardians of the galaxy(2023)

I just added this movie here because i ran out of ideas. This movie was one of the most boring franchise in marvel superhero movies, no offense to Chris pratt. i lowkey love him as an actor but i'm glad they concluded the GOTG franchise quick. i dont recommend it, respectfully.
